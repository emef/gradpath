What is MGP?

MyGradPath is an entirely web based application to help bridge the gap between students and advisors by allowing for online managment of degree requirement satisfaction.

How do I use MGP?

Please see our brief introductory demo video, availible here.

Who Developed MGP?

MyGradPath was developed over the 2011-2012 school year by Western Washington University students Conner Reeves and Matt Forbes as part of the CS departments's senior project program.
